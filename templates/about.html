ABOUT US

Permanent Press is an independent publicity firm based in Brooklyn, NY. First launched in 2002 by Kathy Reilly, the company has since grown to represent a wide variety of success global music artists. The agency's staff includes highly motivated and qualified public relations professionals, each with first-hand experience handling music artists and publicizing entertainment events throughout the country.
