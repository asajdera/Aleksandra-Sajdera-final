ABOUT

Rita Ora is an international pop singer with multiple #1 singles, and 13 singles in the UK top 10. Her debut album, Ora premiered at #1 on the UK charts and was certified platinum. Recently she hit a major milestone, having the record for the most Top 10 Singles for a British female artist in the UK. Her debut U.S. album Phoenix features 3 top 5 UK platinum singlesand includes “Lonely Together,” her collaboration with Avicii which most recently won Best Dance VMA at the MTV Video Music Awards.Rita’s film credits include the Fifty Shades of Grey franchise, Fast & Furious, Southpaw and the upcoming Detective Pikachu.
